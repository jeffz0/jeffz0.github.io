Mean Faces

What defines the features of different demographics such as age, ethnicity, or gender? Often, it's hard to pinpoint the exact features that define such demographics. By averaging out several people of the same demographic, the most prominent features become more apparent. Here is work showing the different "average" faces of different demographics!
