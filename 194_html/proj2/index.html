Project 2: Camera Obscura

Leon Liang and Jeffrey Zhang

Overview

A pinhole camera is a lensless camera that allows light to pass through a small hole, which creates an image on the other side. The small hole only allows a few photons to pass through, creating a clearer image.

Source: http://myworldweb.com/?p=218

Pinhole Camera Setup

Box Setup

Originally, we began by creating a box setup to work with a phone camera. However, after taking several pictures, we could not get a bright enough image. Thus, we decided to borrow a dslr camera to capture the images in our pinhole camera.

We used a 11'' by 8-1/2'' by 5-1/2'' box and lined one face inside the box with several sheets of white paper. This will be our image projection side. We lined the remaining faces inside the box with black paper to prevent any reflection of light, which would blur our image. The box was thoroughly taped with black electrical tape (inside and out) to prevent any light leakage.
