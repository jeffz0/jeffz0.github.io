Welcome!

I am a 4th year undergraduate studying Computer Science at UC Berkeley. I am currently doing computer vision research with Professor Rahul Deo (from UCSF) on diagnosing heart diseases from echocardiograms, advised by Pulkit Agrawal and Professor Alexei Efros (at UC Berkeley). Previously, I was advised by Laura Hallock and Professor Ruzena Bajcsy on medical imaging project for building exoskeletons.

My interests lie in computer vision, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. I want to understand how we can better represent knowledge. More importantly, I want to research how humans see and think to inform our computer algorithms to do the same. Feel free to take a look at some of the research and projects I've been working on!
